## How to Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Login page">
    Visit [cactusian.com/login](https://cactusian.com/login) and enter the email address associated with your Cactusian account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your Email">
    An email will be sent to your address with a 6-digit code that you need to enter. If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder and make sure you're on the correct email account, look for any misspellings you might've made to your email when you entered it and lastly try to resend it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're good to go">
    You will be logged into your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Experiencing Issues?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't receive the Verification Email">
    If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder and make sure you're on the correct email account, look for any misspellings you might've made to your email when you entered it and lastly try to resend it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I forgot / lost access to my Account Email">
    If you forgot or lost access to the email address associated with your Cactusian account we recommend requesting assistance through our [Support Center](https://support.cactusian.com). You might have to eventually provide additional information in order for us to verify your identity, if we are unable to do so you may not regain access to your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I am unable to Complete the Captcha">
    If you are experiencing issues while trying to complete your captcha we recommend you try a different captcha (that you can get using the shown captcha controls) or try again later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
