<Note>
  You must be at least 13 years old to sign up for an account. Please consult our [Terms of Service](https://cactusian.com/terms-of-service) and [Privacy Policy](https://cactusian.com/privacy-policy) before continuing.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign Up page">
    Head over to [cactusian.com/sign-up](https://cactusian.com/sign-up) and enter your real personal birthday. Read, acknowledge and agree with the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter & Verify your Email">
    After you proceed, enter your email address and continue. You will be asked to verify your email address by entering a 6-digit code that was sent to you. If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder and make sure you're on the correct email account, look for any misspellings you might've made to your email when you entered it and lastly try to resend it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a Username">
    It's time to pick your Cactusian username. This is the most important detail of your account, it's what other people will use to find you and engage with you. You can change your username later, but with a 7-day cooldown to prevent abuse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your Profile">
    Before you finish off, you have the option to quickly customize your profile, like changing your profile picture and name. When everything looks good you can continue. Your account will be created and you will automatically be logged in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Experiencing Issues?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't receive verification email">
    If you don't receive the code, check your spam folder and make sure you're on the correct email account, look for any misspellings you might've made to your email when you entered it and lastly try to resend it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My preferred username is taken">
    If your preferred username is taken we recommended choosing a similar or different one. Cactusian is unable to help you with this issue. Usernames are given based on a "first come, first served" system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I am unable to complete the captcha">
    If you are experiencing issues while trying to complete your captcha we recommend you try a different captcha (that you can get using the shown captcha controls) or try again later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
